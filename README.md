###Customer Segmentation Using K-Means Clustering
Objective
To analyze customer purchasing behavior and segment retail store customers into meaningful groups using the K-Means Clustering algorithm.
Technologies & Tools

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Scikit-learn
* Google Colab

Project Highlights :

* Performed data exploration and preprocessing.
* Analyzed customer demographics and spending patterns.
* Applied the **Elbow Method** to determine the optimal number of clusters.
* Implemented **K-Means Clustering** to identify distinct customer segments.
* Visualized customer groups based on **Annual Income** and **Spending Score**.
* Derived insights that can support targeted marketing and customer relationship strategies.

Key Insights :

* Customers were successfully segmented into distinct groups based on spending behavior and income levels.
* The analysis identified high-value customers, potential growth customers, and lower-engagement customer segments.
* Customer segmentation can help businesses improve marketing efficiency, customer retention, and overall business decision-making.

Conclusion :

This project provided valuable hands-on experience in **Unsupervised Machine Learning**, **Data Visualization**, and **Customer Analytics**. It reinforced the importance of data-driven decision-making in understanding customer behavior and developing effective business strategies.
